📊 Sales Performance Dashboard Analysis

🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes end-to-end sales performance using a structured dataset of 62K orders, generating actionable insights across revenue, profit, customers, products, and regions. The goal is to help stakeholders make data-driven business decisions by identifying high-performing segments and optimization opportunities.

---

📈 Key Metrics

- Total Revenue: $1.19B
- Total Profit: $443.9M
- Total Orders: 62K
- Revenue per Order: $19.27K

---

💡 Key Insights

1. Revenue & Profit Trends

- Monthly trends show consistent revenue growth with seasonal fluctuations.
- Profit follows a similar pattern, indicating stable margins across months.

2. Product Performance

- Top revenue-generating products (e.g., Product 26, 25, 13) contribute significantly to overall sales.
- High-margin products (e.g., Product 9, 30, 28) maintain ~38–40% profit margins, indicating premium pricing or cost efficiency.
- Insight: High revenue ≠ high margin, so product strategy should balance both.

3. Channel Analysis

- Wholesale dominates with ~54% of total revenue and ~53% of profit.
- Distributor channel contributes ~31%.
- Export channel, while smaller (~15%), shows highest profit margin (~38%).
- Insight: Export is a high-efficiency channel worth scaling.

4. Customer Insights

- Revenue is concentrated among top customers like Aibox Company and State Ltd.
- However, top profit-margin customers differ, indicating pricing or discount variations.
- Insight: Opportunity to optimize pricing strategy for high-revenue customers.

5. Regional Performance

- West region leads (~30% revenue), followed by South and Midwest.
- Northeast contributes the least (~17%).
- Insight: Potential to expand in underperforming regions.

6. Order Value Distribution

- Majority of orders fall within lower value bins.
- Insight: Increasing average order value can significantly boost revenue.

---

🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Data Visualization: Power BI / Tableau
- Data Processing: Excel / SQL
- Data Modeling: KPI design, aggregation, segmentation

---

🎯 Business Recommendations

- Focus on high-margin products to improve profitability.
- Expand export channel for better margins.
- Improve customer-level pricing strategies.
- Target low-performing regions with marketing campaigns.
- Increase average order value through bundling or upselling.

---

🚀 Outcome

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Track performance in real time
- Identify growth opportunities
- Make strategic, data-backed decisions
